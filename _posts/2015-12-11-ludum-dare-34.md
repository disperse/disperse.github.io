Here are some ideas I might have used *if I had time*:

## Parent simulator, working vs. spending time with family ##
+ Two buttons
    + Go left: go to work and make money
    + Go right: stay home and spend time with your child
+ Child grows up from baby to young child to young adult
+ Spend more time with the child and they are
    + Happy
    + Well adjusted
+ Spend too little time with your child and they are
    + Unhappy
    + Get in trouble
+ Spend more time at work and buy new stuff!
+ Spend too little time at work and the house starts to fall apart 
+ Maybe more like discrete choices?
    + Boss asks you to work late on a presentation
    + Child has a baseball game

## Baseball game, steroid use ##
+ You are a struggling power hitter during the steroid era of baseball
+ Three game modes
    + You're at the plate trying to get a home run: power / contact
    + You're at the gym and are offered steroids: yes / no
    + Working out at the gym: alternate buttons to work out
